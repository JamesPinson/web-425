Composer

Claude Debussy was born on August 22, 1862 and died March 25, 1918. He was a famous french composer who developed a highly original system of harmony and musical structure. His major works include Clair de lune, prelude a lapres-midi dun faune, pelleas et Melisande. His main musical influences were Richard Wagner and Aleksandr Borodin. His work was a seminal force in the music of the 20th century.
